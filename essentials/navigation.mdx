Playpulse Panel offers a revolutionary backup system to ensure your data is always safe and recoverable.

## Automated Backup Solutions

*   **Scheduled backups:** Set up automatic backups to run at regular intervals.
*   **Manual backup creation:** Create on-demand backups whenever necessary.
*   **Multi-cloud storage (AWS S3):** Store your backups securely in the cloud.
*   **Incremental backups:** Efficiently back up only the changes made since the last backup.
*   **Encryption & compression:** Protect your data with encryption and save space with compression.

## Lightning-Fast Restore

*   **One-click restoration:** Restore your servers to a previous state with a single click.
*   **Multiple restore points:** Choose from various restore points to find the exact version you need.
*   **Zero-downtime migration:** Migrate your servers without any interruption to service.
